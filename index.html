07 / The rules

Immutable by design.
Not policy. Mathematics.

◈
One inscription per life

One human being. One pixel. Irrevocable. Biometric liveness validation at the moment of sealing — verified on-chain, no data retained.

◈
Maximum 5 pixels per person

In the primary sale. One for you. Up to four to gift. No hoarding. No speculation at scale.

◈
Cycles are immutable

2050 and 2060 are block heights inscribed at deploy. No DAO, no court, no catastrophe can alter the revelation schedule.

◈
The protocol has no owner

Administrative keys are burned in 2060. The creator exits entirely. What remains is mathematics, community, and time.

Anti-speculation architecture · 4 structural layers · inscribed at deploy · cannot be removed
⬡
Price ceiling

$1,226 hard ceiling. Whales cannot buy the protocol. The formula is deterministic, public, and on-chain from day one.

⬡
5-pixel limit

Maximum 5 pixels per person in the primary sale. Concentration of supply is structurally impossible.

⬡
Unanchored escalating table

Pixels acquired outside the protocol escalate in reconciliation cost — penalizing speculation and protecting legitimate holders.

⬡
Payment independence

The payment layer and the permanence layer are completely independent. You pay in any currency. The record is always on Bitcoin.

◙
Blind Market

On the secondary marketplace, position, zone, and pixel ID are never shown to the buyer. You discover what you bought when you open it.

00WE ARE ALL TEMPORARY

You are going to die. Everyone you know is going to die. Every building, every city, every civilization will eventually crumble and be forgotten. This is not a threat. It is the oldest truth.

And yet, humanity has always tried to leave something behind. Cave paintings. Stone temples. Letters sealed inside walls. Diaries buried in gardens. Messages written for children not yet born.

The desire to be remembered is not vanity. It is the deepest human instinct — the knowledge that existence matters, that presence must leave a trace, that someone was here.

SN is that trace. Built for the digital era. Designed to outlast it.
01WHAT SN IS

SN is not an NFT. Not a marketplace. Not a token. Not a financial instrument.

SN is a human registry protocol. A permanent, decentralized record of existence — each entry representing one human being at a specific point in time and space.

Ten million pixels. Ten million lives. One grid. One experiment. One archive that will outlast everyone who contributed to it.

“A pixel is a time capsule. The value is not in the pixel itself — it is in the timeline of eternals it contains.”
11THE PROTOCOL HAS NO OWNER

SN will govern alongside the community during the first cycle, with decreasing power over thirty years. Veto rights expire automatically in three years. Administrative keys are burned in thirty years. In 2060, SN will have left the project completely.

“If time were flexible, SN would be a product. If content were editable, SN would be a draft. Without these two truths, there is no time capsule — only common storage.”

In 2150, when the fourth cycle closes, the world will be unrecognizable compared to today. The people reading the grid will not know us. And they will be able to look at a pixel and see: here was a human being. This is what they thought. This is where they were. This is what they left behind.
